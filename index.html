About Ricker Milano

Ricker Milano is a transformational coach, author, and advocate for feminine empowerment. With over a decade of experience in personal development and healing arts, she has guided thousands of women toward their sovereign selves.

Her approach blends ancient wisdom with modern psychology, creating a unique framework for personal transformation that honors both strength and softness.

Through her work, Ricker creates sacred spaces where women can reclaim their power, heal their wounds, and step into their fullest expression of self-sovereignty.
